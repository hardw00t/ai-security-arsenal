## WORKFLOW_STEP_BYPASS
# Multi-step flows (checkout, KYC, registration, password reset).
# Probes:
#  - Submit final step directly without intermediate steps
#  - Re-order steps (step3 → step1 → step4)
#  - Replay completed step with stale token to reset/branch state
#  - Tamper server-recorded step id in client storage
